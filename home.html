A library is a very useful platform that brings together people willing to learn. It helps us in learning and expanding our knowledge. VISION

To establish the library as a centre for excellence in transdisciplinary domains and to inculcate the spirit of life-long learning among the stake holders by providing right information to the user at the right time.

MISSION

M1. To provide ethical and conducive ambience to refer various resources.

M2. To ensure that the library has the latest edition of books and e-resources

M3. To encourage stakeholders to develop multidisciplinary skills for self-learning.

M4. To provide quality learning experience by connecting globally to other knowledge centres.
